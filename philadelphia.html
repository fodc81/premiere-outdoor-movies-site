About Our Philadelphia Service

Philly's Outdoor Cinema Experts

Premiere Outdoor Movies has been delivering unforgettable outdoor cinema experiences throughout the greater Philadelphia area since 2009. From block parties in Fishtown to corporate events on the Main Line, from school movie nights in Delaware County to community festivals in South Jersey, we know this market and we know it well.

Our Philadelphia team brings a full production setup to every event: a towering inflatable screen, a high-lumen laser projector, a professional sound system, a generator, and an experienced operator who handles everything from setup to teardown. Your job is to show up and enjoy the show.

We serve all of Philadelphia city, Delaware County, Montgomery County, Bucks County, eastern Chester County up to Route 202, and the South Jersey suburbs across the Delaware River including Cherry Hill, Voorhees, Moorestown, Medford, and more. If you are within our territory, we have you covered.
