About Our New York Service

NYC's Outdoor Cinema Experts

Premiere Outdoor Movies has been delivering professional outdoor cinema experiences throughout the greater New York area since 2009. From rooftop screenings in Manhattan to community movie nights in Brooklyn, from HOA events across Westchester to summer block parties on Long Island, we have the experience and equipment to make any event exceptional.

Our New York team brings a complete production setup to every event: a towering inflatable screen, a high-lumen laser projector, commercial-grade audio, a generator, and an experienced on-site operator who handles everything from arrival to teardown. New York events demand a certain standard — we meet it every time.

We serve all five NYC boroughs, Westchester County, Nassau and Suffolk counties on Long Island, and the Hudson Valley. We understand the unique logistics of urban event production, including power access, space constraints, and permit coordination. If you are within our territory, we have the experience to make it work.
