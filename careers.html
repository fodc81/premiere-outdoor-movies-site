The Role

What You'll
Be Doing

Our event technicians are the face of Premiere Outdoor Movies on-site. You'll handle the setup, run the show, and wrap it up — while making sure every guest has a great time.

  • Load and transport equipment to event locations in your vehicle
  • Set up inflatable screens, projectors, and audio systems on-site
  • Greet clients and guests in a friendly, professional manner
  • Operate the projection and audio system throughout the event
  • Troubleshoot any on-site issues quickly and calmly
  • Break down and reload all equipment after the event
  • Represent the Premiere Outdoor Movies brand with pride

What We're Looking For

  • Friendly, people-oriented personality — you enjoy interacting with customers
  • Reliable and punctual — events run on tight timelines
  • Comfortable with physical setup work (lifting, assembling equipment)
  • Basic technical aptitude — if you can troubleshoot your own phone, you'll be fine
  • Professional appearance and demeanor at all times
  • Able to work evenings and weekends as needed
  • Strong communication skills and a customer-first attitude
🚙 Applicants must have access to an SUV or light truck — required to transport equipment to events.
