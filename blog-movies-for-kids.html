Movies for Kids

By Premiere Outdoor Movies · September 1, 2022

  • Movies for Kids Click for Your Local Premiere Office Phone Number Movies for Kids August 30, 2022 Are you looking for some movies to watch with your kids? Look no further! Here is a list of upcoming movies for kids that will surely be a hit with the whole family. Animated Films

Coco is the upcoming animated movie from the creative minds of Disney and Pixar. This film is set on Mexican culture's stunningly beautiful and vibrant world. The movie follows the story of Miguel, a young boy who dreams of becoming a famous musician despite his family's generations-old ban on music. He embarks on an extraordinary journey filled with music, laughter, and adventure. Coco is sure to capture the imagination of young and old alike.&nbsp;

2. How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World:&nbsp;

The latest in the How to Train Your Dragon trilogy is sure to be a hit with the kids. A few years after the events of the second movie, the story follows Hiccup and his dragon, Toothless, as they search for the mythical Hidden World, a haven for dragons. Along the way, they face many obstacles and discover secrets that could change the world forever.

The Second Part: The return of the Lego movie franchise is sure to be a hit with the kids. The movie follows Emmet, Lucy, Batman, and their friends as they battle a new enemy, The Duplo invaders. This movie is filled with action, adventure, and plenty of laughs.&nbsp;

4. Ralph Breaks The Internet:

Ralph Breaks The Internet is the sequel to the hit movie Wreck-It Ralph. Taking place six years after the first movie's events, Ralph and Vanellope embark on a journey into the uncharted world of the internet in search of a spare part for Vanellope's game. Along the way, they make new friends and encounter unexpected obstacles.

Movie fans of all ages can look forward to the latest lineup of live-action movies for kids hitting the big screen. With various genres, tones, and stories, there is sure to be something for everyone! Here are some of the top live-action movies that children and adults alike will be sure to enjoy.

1. Tom and Jerry:&nbsp;

Tom and Jerry are back in action as they face off in a live-action adaptation of the beloved cartoon. Starring Chlo&euml; Grace Moretz and Michael Pe&ntilde;a, audiences can expect a heart-warming and comedic adventure as Tom and Jerry must outsmart each other to save the day. With great action sequences and a fresh take on the classic characters, Tom and Jerry will surely be a hit!&nbsp;

The Witches, an adaptation of Roald Dahl's classic children's book, tells the story of a young boy and his grandmother as they take on a coven of child-hating witches. With an all-star cast led by Anne Hathaway and Octavia Spencer, this darkly humorous fantasy adventure is sure to be a treat for viewers of all ages.&nbsp;

3. Peter Rabbit 2, The Runaway:

With a mix of colorful characters and plenty of hijinks, Peter Rabbit 2: The Runaway is sure to be an entertaining and delightful family adventure.&nbsp;

The King's Man is a prequel to the Kings man movies that follows a group of aristocrats as they face off against a group of powerful villains.

With a star-studded cast and an exciting storyline, The King's Man is sure to be an action-packed and thrilling ride.&nbsp;

  • Superhero Movies for Kids

Kids and adults love superhero movies, so there's plenty of hype surrounding what Marvel will bring to the big screen. With the promise of new adventures and thrilling battles, the four Marvel movies will capture the hearts of the whole family.

Black Widow will kick off the year, and it's sure to be a hit with any fan of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Starring Scarlett Johansson, this prequel will take us back to the character's origins and explore what makes her the hero she is. With plenty of action and spy-thriller vibes, this movie will surely be a hit for kids and adults alike.

2. Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings:

Directed by Destin Daniel Cretton, this movie will feature the first Asian superhero in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, played by Simu Liu.&nbsp;

This movie follows the story of Shang-Chi as he discovers he is the son of a powerful criminal and must use his martial arts skills to save the world. Full of thrills and excitement, this movie will keep kids of all ages on the edge of their seats.

The Eternals will take us on an epic journey through space and time. Directed by Chlo&eacute; Zhao, this movie focuses on a group of immortal aliens who have been living on Earth secretly for centuries. With an impressive cast that includes Angelina Jolie, Richard Madden, and Kit Harington, this movie will be packed with action and adventure and will surely be a hit with the whole family.

4. Spider-Man: No Way Home:

In the last Marvel movie released, Tom Holland will reprise his role as Peter Parker in Spider-Man: No Way Home. With the promise of a thrilling storyline and plenty of web-slinging action, this movie will be an exciting adventure for kids of all ages. It's sure to be a hit with fans of the original Spider-Man trilogy and those new to the series.

  • Sci-Fi Movies for Kids

The world of science fiction movies is ever-evolving, especially when it comes to movies designed for young viewers. This year is an awe-inspiring year for those looking for the perfect sci-fi movie to entertain and engage their children. Here are some of the most exciting upcoming movies for kids.

The long-awaited sequel to James Cameron's groundbreaking Avatar will hit theaters, and it promises to be an even more thrilling and visually stunning follow-up than the original.

With an ensemble cast that includes Zoe Saldana, Sam Worthington, Sigourney Weaver, and Kate Winslet, the movie follows Jake and Neytiri as they wage a battle against a new enemy on the forbidden planet of Pandora.&nbsp;

2. Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3:

The highly anticipated third installment of the hugely popular Guardians of the Galaxy series sets to hit theaters.&nbsp;

With Chris Pratt and other members of the original cast set to return, the movie follows the Guardians on an exciting new adventure as they attempt to save the universe from an unknown threat.&nbsp;

The Tomorrow War is a science fiction action-thriller set in 2051 when a group of soldiers travels back to fight a war against an unknown, futuristic enemy. The movie stars Chris Pratt, Yvonne Strahovski, J.K. Simmons, and Betty Gilpin and is sure to be an exciting, action-packed thrill ride for viewers of all ages.&nbsp;

4. The Moon and the Sun:

The Moon and the Sun is an upcoming fantasy adventure movie based on the novel by Vonda N. McIntyre.&nbsp;

The film follows a young girl who discovers an ancient sea creature that has the power to grant immortality. It stars Pierce Brosnan and William Hurt and is sure to be a captivating, magical tale for viewers of all ages.

From animated films like Coco to superhero films like Black Widow, this year is sure to be a great year for kids' movies! There's something for everyone, whether they prefer live-action films like Tom and Jerry or science fiction films like Avatar 2. All in all, there are plenty of exciting movies to watch, no matter what kind of film your kids prefer.

Yes, our audio systems are fully adjustable. Our technicians calibrate volume to ensure it's comfortable for all audience members, including little ones.
