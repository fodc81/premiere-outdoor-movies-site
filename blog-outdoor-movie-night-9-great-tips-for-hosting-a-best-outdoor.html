Outdoor Movie Night: 9 Great Tips For Hosting A Best Outdoor Movie Night

By Premiere Outdoor Movies · October 1, 2023

  • Outdoor Movie Night: 9 Great Tips For Hosting A Best Outdoor Movie Night Click for Your Local Premiere Office Phone Number Outdoor Movie Night: 9 Great Tips For Hosting A Best Outdoor Movie Night February 28, 2021 There is nothing better than getting comfy, enjoying some delicious popcorn, and watching a good movie with your family and friends. With modern technology, you do not have to compromise on quality when organizing the best outdoor movie night. Outdoor movie nights are great for any event and fun for everyone. Here are 9 great tips for planning a best outdoor movie night. Best Outdoor Movie Night Planning Before hosting a movie night, you should plan the movie spot, movie day, and how to decorate it. Make your outdoor movie planning simple yet creative. It's much more important to reminisce with loved ones than to have all the details in order. To plan a memorable outdoor movie night, there are a few things to consider:
  • Outdoor Movie Night Location Find out where to organize your great outdoor movie night. When you choose your event spot, keep in mind the following:
  • What time is the sunset on that day?
  • Is there enough parking space?
  • Is it easily accessible?
  • Are restrooms available during the event?
  • Does your spot have enough space for all guests? The event type will also help determine the location for your movie. Consider an outdoor movie night with your colleagues. Or you can enjoy a movie night with your school friends and parents. Outdoor movie nights in apartment communities are entertaining for people of all ages.
  • Movie Selection for the Outdoor Movie Night Try to choose something family-friendly and not too noisy so as not to disturb the neighbors. Reduce conflict by choosing a movie before some days.&nbsp;
  • It will also help you plan your night theme.
  • Consider your audience and try selecting a movie that everyone will love and enjoy.&nbsp;
  • Follow A Theme to Make the Event Memorable Choose a theme based on your selected movie or the time of year you organize the outdoor event. Consider one of these features for your beautiful outdoor movie night:
  • Outdoor Movie Night Underwater Theme
  • Outdoor Movie Night Humanitarian Theme
  • Outdoor Movie Night Halloween Theme
  • Set up an Outdoor Movie Night Theater: Projector, Screen, Speakers Some equipment is needed to bring out the theater experience. Ensure the availability of the following things:
  • Projector Setup: &nbsp;A projector is a must when it comes to outdoor movie nights. Select a projector that provides a bright, clear image and can be controlled remotely. Plus, it should be compatible with a laptop, SD card, or other devices, so you can watch your movie no matter how it is stored or streamed.&nbsp;
  • Screen Setup: &nbsp;Depending on your outdoor setup, you can project the movie on the side of your garage or house. If these structures are made of something overly textured, such as siding or brick, a portable screen could be a better choice. Select a screen that is height adjustable. And a bigger screen is the best choice.
  • Speakers Setup: &nbsp;If your projector does not have several built-in speakers, you will probably want to connect an external speaker so that all your guests can hear the movie. Consider buying high-quality speakers. A portable speaker is a great option as it can be connected to devices via Bluetooth or audio jack and supplied with batteries, so you do not have to worry about plugging it.
  • Extension Cords: &nbsp;If your projector or speakers are not battery-operated, make sure you have some extension cords on hand for access from the nearest port to the device.&nbsp;
  • Make the Perfect Outdoor Movie Seating Make sure you relax easily with these items:
  • Get a Bunch of Pillows &amp; Blankets &nbsp;to create a picnic-like environment in your yard.
  • Bring out Patio or Folding Camping Chairs &nbsp;if you set up your outdoor movie night in a driveway because the blankets may not be soft enough to soften the ground.
  • Use Citronella Candles &amp; Bug Spray&nbsp; to keep mosquitoes and other insects from buzzing in your ears. You can even put a pot of bug repellant plants like basil, lavender, rosemary, and mint on top.
  • Set Walkway Lights or Decorative Mini-Lanterns &nbsp;in a row on trees or movie screen anchors. This will help provide just enough light for guests who want to move around without being distracted from the movie. These essential products cover your entire area to make a beautiful outdoor movie night.
  • Serve Movie Theater Classics: Popcorn, Snacks Candies, Soft Drinks The fact that you offer refreshments to your immediate family makes the job a lot easier. You do not have to worry about reassuring all your guests or making multiple batches! Here is what to offer:
  • Pop Snack-Sized Popcorn Bags &nbsp;and mix-ins such as salt, clarified butter, powered ranch, caramel sauce, cinnamon sugar, or grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Mini Candy Bars satisfy your guests with theater candies like Sno Caps, Twizzlers, Swedish Fish, Junior Mints, and more. Place the candies in a bunch of different glass jars.
  • Serve Bottled Water &amp; Soft Drinks &nbsp;from a cooler filled with ice to make sure they stay cold even after the movie ends. Serve beer and other adult drinks in a separate cooler.
  • Any Outdoor Movie Night Snack &nbsp;like cheese fries, Hassel back kielbasa with dipping sauces, trail mix popcorn, dark chocolate-dipped bananas, low-calorie sliders, frozen lemon sandwich cookies, steak nachos.
  • Stick to Disposable Plates &amp; Napkins &nbsp;to make cleaning breeze.
  • Provide Games to Increase the Fun of Outdoor Movie Night While the movie is the main event, some of the following activities can add to the enjoyment of the evening:
  • Before starting the movie, you can play movie trivia or charades while waiting for the guests to arrive.
  • You can also have video games, karaoke, or a photo booth to create fun memories with your guests.
  • Hand out glow sticks - glow sticks or luminous bracelets can help your guests see in the dark. Kids can play and run around glow sticks to release the energy they built up during the night.
  • After the Event The fun can continue after the movie ends. Your guests may want to chat or eat something. Think of ways to entertain your guests until they go home:
  • Try to keep the momentum of your outdoor movie night from start to finish.
  • Think of innovative ways to accommodate the guests all night long to make it a memorable event.

Whether you're creating a romantic evening for the two, throwing the birthday party, or just hosting a get-together for your friends and family, an outdoor movie night has all the magic and charm you'll fall in love with.&nbsp;

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book an outdoor movie event?
We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance, especially during spring and summer peak season. Last-minute bookings may be accommodated depending on availability, so it's always worth reaching out.
What size screen do I need for my event?
Screen size depends on audience size and viewing distance. A 12–16 ft screen works for 50–100 guests; a 20 ft screen suits 100–200; for larger crowds, 24 ft and up is ideal. We help you choose the right size during your consultation.
What happens if it rains on the day of my event?
We monitor weather closely and communicate with you beforehand. Many of our screens are rated for light wind, and we can often reschedule at no extra charge if severe weather forces a cancellation.
