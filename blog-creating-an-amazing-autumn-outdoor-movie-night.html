By Premiere Outdoor Movies · October 1, 2023

  • Creating An Amazing Autumn Outdoor Movie Night Click for Your Local Premiere Office Phone Number Creating An Amazing Autumn Outdoor Movie Night Premiere Outdoor Movies &bull; October 4, 2019 Cooler, crisper fall nights are on the way and its the perfect time to host an outdoor autumn movie night under the star-filled skies with friends and family. The best part is that it can be a casual and comfortable fall festivity and easy to pull together. Whether you invite family, friends, children, and neighbors there are a variety of options that will inspire you to make hosting a cozy outdoor fall movie night a new tradition. Creating An Amazing Autumn Outdoor Movie Night Concession Stand. What is a fall movie night without a concession stand? Kids and adults love to pick at snacks as they are watching a great movie. A few fun ideas may include a popcorn bar with toppings, caramel apples, hot soups, various fall pies, donuts, and warm cider or hot chocolate.
  • Decoration And Lighting. Spice up the party with an amazing fall display that's easy and practical, too. Pumpkins and corn stalks make for a perfect fall decoration. Lanterns are a stylish way to light a walkway or place them around each party station. Since it turns darker earlier in the fall string lights and mason jar luminaries can work to shed some light, as well as some give your gathering a cozy atmosphere.
  • Adult Beverages. You can find some tasty fall selections of seasonal brews available to keep with the autumn theme and invite your adult guests to crack open a cold one. An arrangement of seasonal brews nestled on ice lets guests pick and choose which to sip, including hoppy lagers and hard ciders.
  • Seating. Use all available outdoor furniture for guest seating. If you need additional seating, consider festive bales of hay arranged with soft blankets for your guest.
  • Cozy Campfire. Consider using a portable fire pit to set the stage for a magical and warmer nighttime gathering. For a classic sweet treat, toast marshmallows after dinner or have the fixings for delicious smores!
  • Activity For Children. If children are invited, consider some activities to keep the party fun and interesting before or after the movie shows. Have a fall cookie decorating station, arts, and crafts station filled with fall activities as well as painting mini pumpkins and then use those pumpkins for a mini pumpkin hunt! Simply place the painted mini pumpkins around the party area for the little ones to find!
  • The Movie. Finding the perfect fall movie should be your only challenge with so many options to choose from. Once that task is complete, let us take care of the details! Bring the magic of movies under the stars to your own backyard. This is one of our most popular events for parties of 5 to 75 people. Show your favorite movies with our high definition movie projection on a state-of-the-art, two-story outdoor movie screen rentals and a concert grade sound system. We can change any size backyard into an entertainment venue that your guests will be talking about all year. If you&rsquo;d like to make your event even more special, you can add music, gaming, and fresh popcorn from one of our antique-style popcorn machines.

Fall is the perfect time for your next movie night with cooler temperatures, great seasonal food options, and fewer bugs! So gather your friends and family and create your own autumn movie night and plan to make life long memories!

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you host an outdoor movie night in cold weather?
Absolutely. With the right preparation — warm blankets, a fire pit, and hot cocoa — cold-weather screenings can be incredibly cozy. We recommend temperatures above 35°F for comfortable outdoor viewing.
What wind speeds are safe for an inflatable movie screen?
Our inflatable screens are anchored and rated for winds up to 15–20 mph. We monitor forecasts and will advise if conditions may be unsafe.
When does it get dark enough for an outdoor movie?
Darkness varies by season. In summer, you may need to wait until 8:30–9 PM for full darkness. In fall and winter, darkness arrives earlier, making for earlier screenings.
Do you offer winter outdoor movie packages?
Yes! Winter screenings can be magical. We provide reliable cold-weather equipment and recommend cozy additions like heated seating areas and warm refreshments.
