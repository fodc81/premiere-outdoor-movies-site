The Opportunity

Why North Carolina?

North Carolina is one of the fastest-growing states in the country — and the demand for premium outdoor entertainment is booming. From the Research Triangle to the Charlotte metro, coastal communities along the Outer Banks to mountain resort towns in Asheville, this state offers an extraordinary variety of event markets waiting to be served.

Charlotte Metro

The largest city in NC with a fast-growing suburban sprawl — HOAs, corporate campuses, and community events are abundant.

Research Triangle (Raleigh/Durham/Chapel Hill)

One of the most educated, tech-forward metro areas in the Southeast. Rich in corporate, university, and community event opportunities.

Greensboro / Winston-Salem / High Point

The Triad — a large metro with a strong mix of corporate, school, and community events.

Wilmington & Cape Fear Coast

A booming coastal market with beach communities, resort venues, and year-round event demand.

Asheville & Western NC

A thriving tourism and events destination with a culture that loves unique, memorable experiences.

Outer Banks

A premium summer market serving vacation communities, resorts, and private events along one of the East Coast's most famous coastlines.
