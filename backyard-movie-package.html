Common Questions

Backyard Party FAQ

How much space do I need in my backyard?

+
The AirScreen Nano is 13 feet wide overall and 12 feet tall. You'll need roughly 20–25 feet of clearance in front of the screen for seating, plus space behind the screen for anchor stakes. Most standard suburban backyards accommodate this setup easily. When you book, our team will confirm the dimensions work for your specific yard.

What movie can I play? Do you provide the content?

+
We bring the equipment — you choose the movie. You can play any DVD using our included DVD player, or play a downloaded video file from a laptop or tablet with an HDMI output. If you plan to stream (Netflix, Disney+, etc.), you'll need a strong WiFi signal at your event location. Keep in mind that public screenings of commercial films may require a public performance license — we're happy to point you in the right direction.

What if it rains?

+
Light rain is typically manageable with the screen in place, but we don't operate in heavy rain, lightning, or high wind conditions. We work with every client to reschedule if weather becomes an issue — we're flexible and understand that outdoor events depend on the weather cooperating.

Do I need to provide anything other than power?

+
Just a standard electrical outlet accessible from the setup area. We bring everything else — screen, projector, audio, cables, DVD player, and all hardware. If you're streaming content wirelessly, a strong WiFi signal is also needed on your end.

How long does setup take, and does the technician stay?

+
Our technician arrives 60–90 minutes before your event start time and stays on-site for the entire event. After the movie ends, they handle full breakdown and removal — typically 30–45 minutes. You don't have to lift a finger.

How far in advance should I book?

+
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for summer weekends which fill up fast. That said, we do accommodate last-minute requests based on availability — reach out and we'll do our best to make it happen.
