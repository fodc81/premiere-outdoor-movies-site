Our Story

Built on a Passion for Outdoor Cinema

Premiere Outdoor Movies was founded in 2009 with a clear mission: bring professional-grade outdoor cinema to communities, families, and businesses — without the complexity or cost of a Hollywood production. What started as a small operation in New Jersey quickly grew into something much bigger.

Word spread fast. HOAs and community associations discovered that an outdoor movie night was the perfect way to bring neighbors together. Schools found that a backyard-style movie fundraiser could outperform a bake sale ten times over. Corporate clients realized that a movie under the stars made for an unforgettable team event. And families found out that a backyard movie night was the kind of memory their kids would talk about for years.

Today, Premiere Outdoor Movies operates across 10 states through a network of owner-operators who share our values — professionalism, reliability, and a genuine love for the craft of outdoor cinema. Every operator is trained to the same standards, uses the same professional-grade equipment, and delivers the same exceptional experience that built our reputation.

We've never lost sight of what made us special: we're not just a rental company. We're a full-service team that shows up, sets up, runs your show, and breaks everything down — so you can focus entirely on your guests.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

09
2009
Premiere is Born
Premiere Outdoor Movies launches in New Jersey with a single inflatable screen and a vision to bring professional outdoor cinema to communities and families. The first events are neighborhood movie nights — and the response is immediate.
12
2012
Corporate & School Events Take Off
Word spreads to schools, HOAs, and corporate clients. Premiere begins producing larger events — school fundraisers, company picnics, and community drive-ins — and invests in bigger screens and commercial audio systems.
15
2015
Multi-State Expansion
Demand from neighboring states leads to Premiere's first geographic expansion. New operators join the network in Pennsylvania, New York, and Connecticut, bringing the Premiere experience to new markets.
18
2018
Laser Projector Upgrade
Premiere transitions its entire fleet to professional laser projectors — a major investment that delivers dramatically brighter, sharper images and positions the company as the clear quality leader in outdoor cinema.
20
2020
Drive-In Revival
When the world went outdoors, Premiere was ready. Drive-in events become one of our most in-demand services, and we expand our capacity and operator network to meet the surge in community and private drive-in bookings.
