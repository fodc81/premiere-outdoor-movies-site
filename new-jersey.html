About Our NJ Service

New Jersey's Outdoor Movie Experts

Premiere Outdoor Movies has been producing professional outdoor cinema events across New Jersey since 2009. What started as a passion for bringing the magic of outdoor movies to families and communities has grown into New Jersey's most trusted outdoor movie screen rental service.

We serve every corner of the Garden State — from the dense suburbs of Bergen and Essex counties in the north, to the Jersey Shore communities of Monmouth and Ocean, through Central Jersey's bustling towns, and all the way down to South Jersey's Cape May and Cumberland counties. No event is too far, no venue is too unusual, and no crowd is too big.

Whether you're planning a neighborhood movie night, a corporate team event, a school fundraiser, or a full-scale drive-in for hundreds of cars, our team handles everything from delivery and setup to operating the system and packing up when it's done. You focus on enjoying the night — we take care of the rest.

Where We Serve

Every Region of New Jersey

We break New Jersey into four natural regions — and we're active in all of them year-round. Here's a closer look at what we do in each area.

🏙️
North Jersey
Bergen · Essex · Passaic · Morris · Hudson · Sussex · Warren

North Jersey is our most active region. Dense suburbs, thriving HOA communities, and a robust corporate event scene make this a year-round hub for outdoor movie events. We regularly produce events at public parks in Bergen County, corporate campuses in Morris County, and neighborhood parties throughout Passaic and Essex.

🌊
Shore Region
Monmouth · Ocean

The Jersey Shore comes alive in summer, and so do outdoor movie events. From Asbury Park to Toms River, beach-town block parties, resort events, and oceanfront community nights are a staple of our summer calendar. Monmouth and Ocean counties are among our busiest from Memorial Day through Labor Day.

🏛️
Central Jersey
Middlesex · Somerset · Mercer · Hunterdon · Union

Central NJ's mix of university towns, busy townships, and corporate corridors generates strong demand for outdoor movie events all year long. We work regularly with schools in Middlesex County, community organizations in Somerset, and corporate clients in the Route 1 corridor through Mercer and Union counties.

🌾
South Jersey
Burlington · Camden · Gloucester · Atlantic · Salem · Cape May · Cumberland

South Jersey — including the Philly suburbs of Camden and Burlington counties, the shore towns of Atlantic and Cape May, and the agricultural communities of Salem and Cumberland — is our crossover territory with Philadelphia. Events here range from sprawling drive-ins on open farmland to family-friendly nights in Atlantic City-area communities.

Backyard Movie Nights

🏫
School Events

🏢
Corporate Events

Employee appreciation nights, client entertainment, and company picnic add-ons for businesses and campuses across North and Central Jersey. Fully branded experiences available.

HOA & Community
