FAQ

Common Questions

Does Premiere serve all of Florida?+
Yes — we serve the entire state of Florida. South Florida, Central Florida, Tampa Bay, Jacksonville, Northeast Florida, Southwest Florida, the Space Coast, and the Panhandle are all within our coverage area.
Can you do outdoor events in Florida year-round?+
Absolutely — Florida's climate is one of the biggest advantages for outdoor events. We operate year-round, and many of our busiest months are October through April when the weather is perfect and the snowbirds are in town.
Do you work with HOAs and condo communities in South Florida?+
Yes — South Florida is one of the most HOA-dense markets in the country and one of our most active Florida territories. We've produced hundreds of community movie nights in planned communities, condos, and gated neighborhoods throughout Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties.
Can you set up at a beach or waterfront venue in Florida?+
Absolutely. Beach and waterfront setups are some of our most popular Florida events. We work with venue coordinators and understand the logistics of sand, wind, and coastal environments.
What is included in every package?+
Every package includes a professional inflatable screen, high-lumen laser projector, commercial-grade audio, a generator, full delivery and setup, and an on-site operator who stays for the entire event and handles teardown.
How far in advance should I book in Florida?+
We recommend 4–8 weeks in advance. Peak season in South Florida runs October through April — the earlier you book for that window, the better. Central and North Florida have strong summer seasons that also book up fast.
