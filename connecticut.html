About Our Connecticut Service

Connecticut's Outdoor Cinema Experts

Premiere Outdoor Movies has been bringing professional outdoor cinema to Connecticut since 2009. From corporate entertaining on Fairfield County's Gold Coast to community movie nights in New Haven, from HOA events in Hartford suburbs to shore town gatherings along the Connecticut coast, we serve the full state with the same professional standard every time.

Connecticut's market is distinctive — the concentration of Fortune 1000 corporations in Fairfield County, the affluent residential communities along the Gold Coast, and the vibrant community life from Stamford to Mystic all create unique demand for high-quality outdoor entertainment. Our team understands these expectations and delivers accordingly.

We serve all of Connecticut: Fairfield County is our strongest territory, but we operate throughout New Haven County, Hartford County, Litchfield County, and Eastern Connecticut including the shoreline communities. Every event gets a full production crew, professional equipment, and our commitment to a flawless experience.
