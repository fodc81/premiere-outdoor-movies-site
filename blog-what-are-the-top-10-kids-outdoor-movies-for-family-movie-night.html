May 20, 2021 What Are The Top 10 Kids' Outdoor Movies For Family Movie Night? Some best kids' outdoor movies were released decades ago. If you are looking for the perfect film to please everyone, we recommend introducing kids to the classic family movies and putting the new releases aside. From Star Wars to Home Alone and everything in between, it's time to enjoy some quality time with your family. Get the popcorn and select a perfect family movie from this list of kids' outdoor movies. 10 Best Kids' Outdoor Movies For A Fun Family Movie Night Here are the top ten kids' outdoor movies for family movie night: 1.&nbsp; &nbsp; Jumanji (PG) Children may relate this movie to the comedy of Dwayne Johnson and Jack Black. But the original movie is much darker.&nbsp; It's based on an award-winning book by Chris Van Allsburg about a cursed board game. Robin Williams' 1995 version is full of real-looking creatures that are a little intense at times. 2.&nbsp; &nbsp; Star Wars (PG) Star Wars is the ultimate sci-fi Hollywood classic. Star Wars implanted itself firmly into pop-culture history. What's the correct order to watch Star Wars films? We suggest starting with the original and continue from there. In its opening shot, a tremendous whooping Star Destroyer whooshes across the screen endlessly. From its opening shot, youngsters will be as enchanted as if they were trapped in a Death Star tractor beam. All the mysteries of the Jedi, scary villains, and timeless heroes share the screen for two hours of matinee-style magic. All this will keep even the most restless youngster busy. Watching the Star Wars movies has become a must for a family movie night with each new addition to the canon and spin-off. Even if you're in the mood for only one trip to a galaxy far away, Star Wars will not let you down. 3.&nbsp; &nbsp; Mary Poppins (G) Mary Poppins is one of the perfect kids' outdoor movies for family movie night. When Jane and Michael Banks make a list of the qualities they want in a nanny, they're pleasantly surprised when Mary Poppins arrives.&nbsp; This movie is full of magical adventures that appeal to everyone's inner child. Mary Poppins Returns (2018) is also one of the perfect kids' outdoor movies for a fun family movie night. Mary Poppins can hardly contain itself with its: Strong supporting cast
  • Battery of dance routines
  • Catalogue of memorable songs

Julie Andrews produced an Academy Award-winning portrayal of the Edwardian nanny. Mary Poppins is a family movie thatis practically perfect in every way.

4.&nbsp; &nbsp; Home Alone (PG)

Who would not want to spend the vacations in the City of Light? The McCallister clan is ready to leave the suburbs behind to spend Christmas in Paris. But, things go slightly wrong when they find out that they left their son Kevin at home.&nbsp;

Kevin has no problem having the house all to himself. He watches mature gangster movies, eats ice cream for dinner, and causes chaos. But, a pair of burglars target the beautiful house. So, Kevin is left to fend for himself against Harry and Marv.&nbsp;

In the lively and more beloved sequel, Kevin is again left behind. But this time, while his family goes on a holiday to Florida, he ends up on a flight to New York. Harry and Marv - the unforgettable burglars - also end up in Gotham.&nbsp;

5.&nbsp; &nbsp; The Wizard of Oz (G)

The Wizard of Oz is a classic among all the classic kids' outdoor movies. For more than 70 years, this movie has captivated generation after generation. A girl trapped on a farm in bleak, sepia-toned Kansas dreams of an exciting life somewhere over the proverbial rainbow.

Her wish comes true when a tornado deposits the girl and her little dog Toto into a technicolor wonderland. This movie's staying power has been attributed to several things. But what always captivates each new generation of youngsters is the vibrancy and charm of the film's fantasy world like:

  • Good witches and flying monkeys
  • Scaredy-cat lions and fleet-footed scarecrows
  • Striking pastel towns of Munchkins and Emerald City&nbsp;

And then there's its timeless message, which states that:&nbsp;

&quot;You can explore the world, have adventures, make new friends, and experience life at its best. But in the end, there is no place like your home and no one like your family and friends.&quot;

That's why millions of people keep returning to this classic with their children. That's also the reason why many more people will keep coming to this family movie for decades.

6.&nbsp; &nbsp; Jurassic Park (PG-13)

Jurassic Park is a thrilling blockbuster family movie. But it needs a premium sound system to truly capture the thrills and magic of its prehistoric roars and scenery.&nbsp;

What can go wrong when you invite three scientists to check out a fictitious theme park filled with real dinosaurs?

7.&nbsp; &nbsp; The Princess Diaries (G)

Mia may not seem a likely candidate for a princess. But it does not make her unworthy of wearing a crown.

The Princess Diaries is a modern-day 'Ugly Duckling' story. It has some unique twists that make it one of the ideal kids' outdoor movies for family movie night .

8.&nbsp; &nbsp; The Princess Bride (PG)

The Princess Bride is a movie of an adventure that delights people of all ages. This movie is the perfect mixture of a love story, adventure, and comedy to please your entire family.

9.&nbsp; &nbsp; Harry Potter Saga (PG-13)

No matter which of the eight Harry Potter movies you select, the children are in for an evening of:

  • Nerve-racking adventure

10.&nbsp; Willy Wonka &amp; the Chocolate Factory (G)

Willy Wonka &amp; the Chocolate Factory is a magical story for sweet-toothed children. This movie tells the story of a little boy from a low-income family. His dream is to get a peek inside Willy Wonka's mysterious factory.&nbsp;

His dream turns into reality eventually. After many children find the Golden Ticket inside their chocolate bars and win a tour of Willy Wonka's factory, it turns out something even more significant is at stake.

We have compiled a list of Disney movies, family comedy movies, '90s throwbacks, and award-winning movies. Select any movie from this list of kids' outdoor movies for a fun family movie night.&nbsp;

Frequently Asked Questions

What movies are most popular for kids' outdoor movie nights?
Classic animated films, superhero movies, and family-friendly blockbusters are always crowd-pleasers. Popular choices include Moana, The Lion King, Frozen, Spider-Man, and Pixar favorites.
How late do kids' events usually run?
For younger audiences, we recommend scheduling the movie to end by 10 PM at the latest. Many families with toddlers choose earlier summer screenings that start around dusk (8–8:30 PM).
Is the sound system adjustable so it's not too loud for young kids?
Yes, our audio systems are fully adjustable. Our technicians calibrate volume to ensure it's comfortable for all audience members, including little ones.
Can we have activities or games before the movie?
Absolutely! Many of our clients set up yard games, face painting, or snack stations before the screening begins. We handle the movie setup so you can focus on the fun.
