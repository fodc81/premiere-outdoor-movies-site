Company Overview

About Premiere

Premiere Outdoor Movies is a full-service outdoor cinema company that has been delivering unforgettable movie experiences since 2009. What started as a passion for bringing people together under the stars has grown into one of the most trusted names in outdoor entertainment across the United States.

We are not a rental company. We are a full-service event production team — professional crews, state-of-the-art equipment, and complete event management from setup to teardown. Every event is handled end-to-end so our clients can focus on their guests, not the logistics.

With over 10,000 events produced and a growing network of owner-operators across 17 states, Premiere Outdoor Movies delivers the same high standard of service whether the event is a 50-person backyard party or a 5,000-person community festival.
